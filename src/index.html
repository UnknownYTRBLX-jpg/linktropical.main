Welcome to FALCON Metalworking

Precision, Technology, Quality

Professional Metalworking and Equipment Rentals

Steel is one of the most commonly used materials in various industries, construction, frameworks and production. For years, we have specialized in professional steel processing, understanding the importance of precision and selecting the right techniques for the desired results. FALCON Metalworking offers professional metalworking services, using state-of-the-art technology and industry expertise.

With advanced machinery and a skilled workforce, we can meet the most demanding needs of our clients. Our services include laser cutting, sheet cutting, welding and other metalworking processes to meet project specifications. Our offerings also include steel powder coating, ensuring comprehensive preparation of steel components.
